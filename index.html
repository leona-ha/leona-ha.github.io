About Me

Hi, I'm Leona Hammelrath! I'm a researcher at Free University Berlin working at the intersection of Machine Learning and Clinical Psychology.

Professionally, I focus on using diverse patient data to monitor psychotherapy and predict therapy outcomes. I have a deep interest in combining data science with mental health research.

I'm currently leading the PREACT-digital project, where we collect long-term digital phenotyping data in 500 patients undergoing CBT for internalizing disorders.
