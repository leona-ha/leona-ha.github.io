About Me

Hi, I'm Leona! I'm a researcher at Free University Berlin, working at the intersection of Machine Learning and Clinical Psychology.

Professionally, I focus on using various types of patient data to monitor psychotherapy and predict therapy outcomes. I have a keen interest in combining data science with mental health research.

I'm currently leading the PREACT-digital project, where we collect digital phenotyping data from patients undergoing CBT for internalizing disorders.
